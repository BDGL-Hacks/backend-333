PartyUp

PartyUp with your friends in a group!

Groups are created around Events. Make a plan to go out and have fun!
Multiple events can be strung together to plan a whole night or weekend.
Groups are erased after the last event so your information is kept private and uncluttered.

Keep track of the people that matter!

The leader of the group sets which events the group should be going to
Other members ‘check in’ to events and can move around by themselves

Chat with friends!

The real time chat for each group helps you keep in touch before, during, or after events
Receive notifications when others are talking

Party safely!

Quickly ping group members to see where they are or if they are ok.
A status check lets you see if people are with the group and responding to pings.

Create events!

Events can be created within a group or by themselves
You can invite users to your event
Attending users can form groups to go to your event
